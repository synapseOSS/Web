Chrome requires PNG icons for PWA installation (SVG not supported for installability).

QUICK FIX:
1. Open generate-png-icons.html in your browser
2. Click "Generate PNG Icons" button
3. Two files will auto-download: icon-192x192.png and icon-512x512.png
4. Move both PNG files to this folder (public/icons/)
5. Restart your dev server
6. Chrome will now show "Install" instead of "Create shortcut"

The manifest.json will automatically use PNG icons once they exist in this folder.
